About the Crooked Path

“A way seems right to a man, but in the end it leads to death.”

Proverbs 14:12

The Crooked Path is a free, family-friendly jump-scare attraction. We lean into the tension of what feels right versus what is true, using atmosphere and timing to deliver clean, startling fun.

Guests wind through an ominous environment cloaked in darkness while ghastly fiends lie in wait. Instead of costumed actors, the experience is driven by custom-built props and effects, operated by volunteers in hidden control rooms for a more convincing scare.

Our desire is to make God’s truth known through this unique outreach—and we hope you find it frightfully entertaining.
