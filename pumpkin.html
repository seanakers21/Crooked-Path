Crooked Cucurbits!

Hey kids! Enter your pumpkin for a chance to win:

  • Most Creative
  • Funniest
  • Scariest
  • Most Classic

Entries will be displayed in the Crooked Path Pumpkin Patch for public voting. Prizes for 1st–3rd in each category!

Contest Rules

  • Contestants: Kids ages 5–10
  • One entry per contestant
  • Entry must be your own design – no templates
  • No inappropriate entries will be allowed
  • Include name, age, and contact info with your entry
  • Entries accepted: Sunday, October 19, 12–5 pm
  • Displayed by volunteers in the Crooked Patch
  • Public voting: Oct 24–25 (Scariest, Funniest, Best Overall)
  • Two brackets: Ages 5–7 & 8–10
  • Winners announced: Oct 31, 3–5 pm
  • Winner photos for website (guardian must be present)
